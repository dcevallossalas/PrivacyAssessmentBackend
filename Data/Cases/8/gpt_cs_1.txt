It also frames processing as a regulated activity subject to the law and establishes the supervisory authority and its role in ensuring compliance, all of which are key \u201ccontext\u201d elements for a PIMS.\n\nCategory 1: (1),(3)(d),(3)(e),(3)(f),(8),(12),(13),(14),(15),(16),(19),(21)  \nI matched these because the law identifies and protects the \u201cTitular\u201d (PII principal) and sets out their rights and interaction mechanisms (consultations, claims, complaints).
